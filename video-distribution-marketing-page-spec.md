## Section-by-section

### 1. Hero
- **H1:** Get your music video everywhere that matters
- **Sub:** Upload once and deliver your video to VEVO, Spotify, Apple Music, TIDAL and Amazon Music — with your own VEVO channel, full monetisation, and 100% of your rights kept.
- **Primary CTA:** `Distribute Your Video` · **Secondary:** `See how it works`
- Visual: screenshot/mock of the multi-store selection step.

### 2. Logo strip — "One upload. Every platform."
Row of platform logos: **VEVO · Spotify · Apple Music · TIDAL · Amazon Music** (+ "and YouTube via VEVO").
*SEO value: names the high-intent platform keywords near the top.*

### 3. Key advantages (3–4 cards — keep it tight)
1. **Your own VEVO channel** — Create a new VEVO channel or link an existing one, and publish straight to YouTube with monetisation and playlist placement.
2. **One upload, every store** — Add your video and thumbnail once; we format and deliver it to every platform you pick.
3. **Keep 100% of your rights & royalties** — You stay the owner. We just get it out there.
4. **Built-in monetisation & analytics** — Earn from views and track performance from the same dashboard as your music.

### 4. "How it works" — 3 steps (mirrors the real builder, simplified)
1. **Upload** your video and thumbnail (or grab a frame straight from the video).
2. **Add details** — metadata, artists, credits and genres, with ISRC handled for you.
3. **Pick stores & schedule** — choose platforms, set your release date, and submit.

> Subtle nod to the product without exposing all 6 internal steps.

### 5. Feature deep-dive (short two-column list, not exhaustive)
- Capture a thumbnail directly from your video
- Schedule a release date + optional timed (to-the-minute) release
- Priority distribution for faster delivery
- Country/territory controls
- Auto ISRC assignment
- Pull metadata from an existing Spotify release to save time

### 6. Social proof / trust band
Stat or testimonial strip (e.g. artists distributed, total views). Placeholder for a quote.

### 7. FAQ (powers FAQ schema → rich results)
- **Where can I distribute my music video?** VEVO, Spotify, Apple Music, TIDAL and Amazon Music — plus YouTube through your VEVO channel.
- **Do I need a VEVO channel already?** No. You can create a new one or link an existing channel during upload.
- **Do I keep my rights?** Yes — you keep 100% ownership and royalties.
- **What video formats are supported?** MP4, MOV and other common HD formats, landscape orientation, up to 2GB.
- **How long until my video goes live?** [fill from real SLA]; priority distribution is available for faster delivery.

### 8. Closing CTA banner
- **H2:** Ready to release your video? · **CTA:** `Get Started`

## Notes for whoever builds it
- **Word count target:** ~450–600 words of body copy — enough for SEO, not a wall of text.
- **One H1, H2s per section**, keyword in the first 100 words and in at least one H2.
- **Internal links:** to music distribution, royalties, and pricing pages (helps topical authority).
- Verify the **2GB / format / SLA** figures against current product limits before publishing — the 2GB and format list come from the uploader; the SLA is a placeholder.
